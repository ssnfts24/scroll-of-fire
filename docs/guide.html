1 · Daily Run

Daily Run (12–18 min total)

  1. Phrase — Eq.16: one line, 8–12 words; verbs of action, nouns of truth; remove negations.
  2. Carrier \( \Pi_{\nu} \): hum 3–5 seconds in the Voice Gate. Stability \( \ge 60\% \), noise low. Presets: 432 / 528 / 369.
  3. Observer loop: 12 seconds × 3 rounds — \( \mathcal{I} \rightarrow \mathcal{L} \rightarrow \mathcal{C} \rightarrow \mathcal{P} \).
  4. \( \Xi \) check: if trending down, apply Eq.9 correction before continuing.
  5. Record: one ledger line: phrase, carrier, coherence score, result, and next correction.

2 · Weekly Rhythm

Seven-Day Review

Once per week, review the ledger. Do not chase signs. Track what repeats, what strengthens peace, and what produces clean action.

  • Keep what increases clarity.
  • Pause what increases confusion.
  • Rewrite phrases that create pressure instead of direction.
  • Move useful observations into the archive.

3 · Weekly Output

One Witness Entry

End the week with one clean witness entry: what was practiced, what changed, what stayed unresolved, and what must be tested next.

4 · Troubleshooting

When the Signal Gets Muddy

Noise high Shorten the phrase. Lower intensity. Return to breath.
Coherence drops Apply Eq.9. Rest, hydrate, ground, and simplify the practice.
Obsession loop Stop recording signs. Do one ordinary task. Return later.
No result Keep the witness clean. Not every seed opens the same day.

5 · Ethics & Stop Rule

Ethical Mask \( \Lambda_{\mathcal{E}} \)

Any practice that increases harm, fear, manipulation, isolation, or loss of grounded judgment must stop and be reviewed.
