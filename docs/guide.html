1) Daily Run (12–18 min total)

  1. Phrase (Eq.16): one line, 8–12 words; verbs of action, nouns of truth; remove negations.
  2. Carrier Πν: hum 3–5s in Voice Gate; Stability ≥ 60%, Noise low. Presets: 432 / 528 / 369.
  3. Observer loop: 12s × 3 rounds – 𝓘 → 𝓛 → 𝓒 → 𝓢 (ring on Teach).
  4. Ξ check: If trending down, apply Eq.9 (stop, re-align, resume).
  5. Witness: log one undeniable detail in the Ledger.
$$ \nabla_{\text{sem}}\text{Phrase}=\sum_k w_k \nabla_{\text{sem}}\text{Token}_k \quad (Eq.16)\qquad \Xi \approx \frac{\langle H\cdot I\rangle}{1+\mathrm{Var}[\mathcal{M}]} $$

2) Weekly Rhythm (τ, t½, λ)

  • Mon–Thu: 1–2 runs/day, same line. Build remanence (ℜ) on purpose.
  • Fri: A/B a carrier or adjust seconds/phase (small ΔΣ only).
  • Sat: Micro-run (90s) + ledger review (sparkline).
  • Sun: Rest / nature / gratitude; let updates settle (⊕).

Spacing inside τ lengthens t½ and lowers λ (decay). Short + steady beats sporadic marathons.

$$ \lambda = \frac{\ln 2}{t_{1/2}}\,,\qquad \text{practice within }\tau \Rightarrow t_{1/2}\uparrow\Rightarrow \lambda\downarrow $$

3) Troubleshooting (fast checks)

  • Foggy witness? Shrink the line; swap “want/try” → “choose”.
  • Wobbly tone? Re-tune carrier; extend exhale; check room noise.
  • Ξ flat? Increase seconds/phase (e.g., 8→12 or 12→20).
  • Overheated? Stop per Eq.9; drink water; walk; resume later.
  • Plateau? Run A/B Protocol or Glyph to refresh salience.

4) Ethics & Stop Rule

Consent, non-harm, accountability. If coherence worsens, abort the run, re-align, resume later.

$$ \text{If}\ \frac{\mathrm{Truth}(t+\Delta)}{\mathrm{Distortion}(t+\Delta)} < \frac{\mathrm{Truth}(t)}{\mathrm{Distortion}(t)} \Rightarrow \text{Abort, Re-align, Resume} \quad (Eq.9) $$
