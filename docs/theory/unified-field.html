Δ0–Δ7

The eight-stage scaffold.

The Δ sequence provides an order for entering, examining, acting within, and leaving a field. Each stage will receive a deeper treatment in the dedicated Delta Framework chapter.

Δ0 Frame

Declare scope, location, time, participants, question, and boundary.

Δ1 Source

Identify available energy, information, materials, conditions, and constraints.

Δ2 Witness

Record what is observed before explanation hardens around it.

Δ3 Gate

Determine what signals, claims, tones, and actions are permitted into the working field.

Δ4 Language

Name the pattern and examine how words alter meaning, attention, and response.

Δ5 Memory

Compare the present pattern with retained experience, archives, habits, and inherited structure.

Δ6 Coherence

Measure the fit among declared purpose, available evidence, behavior, and outcome.

Δ7 Remnant

Preserve what remains useful and true after ethical review, correction, and release.
