# Pattern Calendar v1

## Historical conversion rule (authoritative)

- Every Pattern Year begins on civil **April 17**.
- The counted year is always **364 Pattern Days** (13 moons × 28 days).
- Counted days are followed by intercalary outside days until the next civil April 17.
- The first intercalary day is **Day Out of Time**.
- If the civil interval from April 17 to the next April 17 is 366 days, the second intercalary day is **Deep Time Day**.
- This same rule applies before and after the 2026 reference epoch.
- Conversions use proleptic Gregorian civil dates unless an explicit specification states otherwise.

## Reference epoch

- 2026-04-17 = Pattern Year 1, Moon 1, Day 1, Day 1/364.
