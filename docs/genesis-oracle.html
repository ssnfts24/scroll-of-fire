How the Genesis Oracle reads your birth

  1. Anchor: the Remnant year begins at the first New Moon after Spring Equinox.
  2. Cadence: 13 fixed moons of 28 days create a 364-day counted year.
  3. Outside days: remaining days after Moon 13 sit outside the counted lattice as reset/intercalary days.
  4. Mapping: your birth date maps to Moon 1–13 and Day 1–28, or to an outside-count span.
  5. Tone: the counted index mod 13 gives the Tone of the Day.
  6. Resonant Sum: the digit reduction of YYYY+MM+DD lights the seal on 1, 4, 7, 11, 22, or 33.
