Physical Lift Training

7-Step Lift Loop

Physical alignment practice for posture, breath, balance, nervous-system steadiness, and felt lightness.

1 2 3 4 5 6 7

1 · Grounding Resonance

Feet planted. Slow pulse beneath feet and spine. Settle into support.

2 · Flow Medium

Picture air as layered currents. Notice pressure, warmth, and space.

3 · Body as Resonator

Spine as axis. Inhale up. Exhale down. Smooth jaw, shoulders, ribs.

4 · Phase Lock

Watch breath and posture line up. Notice lighter, steadier balance.

5 · Drift Feedback

Tension is data. Find whether drift began in breath, posture, thought, or effort.

6 · Lift Command

Use the cue: “brighten the center.” Let the body lengthen upward without strain.

7 · Witness & Integration

Pause, breathe normal, record balance, posture, body sensation, emotion, and clarity.
