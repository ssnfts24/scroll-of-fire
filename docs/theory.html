Master Equation

The Living Equation

$$ \boxed{ \text{Reality} = \left( E \times I \times N \times M \times F \right)^{C} } $$

Reality is treated as an emergent process shaped through Energy, Information, Intention, Memory, and Flow, amplified or transformed through Consciousness.

E · Energy

Motion, charge, force, breath, heat, effort, and raw capacity to act.

I · Information

Pattern, code, structure, language, geometry, DNA, number, and meaning.

N · Intention

Will, direction, attention, choice, prayer, desire, and alignment.

M · Memory

Record, inheritance, trauma, archive, testimony, artifact, and trace.

F · Flow

Water, breath, circulation, entropy, electricity, exchange, and movement.

C · Consciousness

The exponent. The participating witness that changes how the whole field is experienced.
