Operator Algebra — Composition Rules

  • Order: \( \mathcal{I}\prec\mathcal{L}\prec\mathcal{C}\prec\mathbb{M}_{\tau}\prec\mathsf{Upd}\prec\mathsf{Mask} \). Re-ordering is not permitted unless proven Ξ-stable.

Practice Protocols & Instrumentation

Field guide for running the loop: Frame → Carriers → Language → Witness → Update → Mask. Every step emits a structured log so results are reproducible and extensible.

1) Framing (Eq 11)

$$ \mathsf{Frame}(x,t)=\big(\mathsf{scope},\ \mathcal{W},\ \mathsf{ethics}\big) $$
  1. Declare one sentence for scope, one for ethics, and one witness method.
  2. Hold one calm breath to “set ink”.
  3. Record a 30–60 s baseline HRV (optional) and the felt clarity/warmth/ease (0–5 each).

Output keys: frame.scope, frame.ethics, frame.witness

2) Carriers & Gate (Eq 17, Eq 3, Eq 6)

$$ \mathbf{V}(\nu)=\sum_{k} a_k \cos(2\pi\nu_k t+\phi_k),\quad \mathcal{H}(\nu)\in\{0,1\} $$
  • Pick 2–4 clean bands (e.g., 432/528/369/144/963) or subharmonics. Start quietly.
  • Align beat envelope (≤6 Hz) with natural breath; then gently phase-lock (PL) to reduce jitter.

Output keys: carriers[].nu, carriers[].a, carriers[].phi, gate.mode

3) Language & Semantic Gradient (Eq 4, 4b)

$$ \nabla_{\mathrm{sem}} \mathrm{Phrase}=\sum_k w_k\,\nabla_{\mathrm{sem}}\mathrm{Token}_k,\quad \sum_k w_k=1 $$
  • Create a 7-word phrase that matches the frame; test two variants; keep the higher-Ξ one.
  • Weigh key tokens with softer onset + longer vowels; keep breath even.

Output keys: phrase.text, phrase.tokens[], phrase.weights[]

4) Witness (Eq 2) with Memory Window (Eq 16)

$$ \mathbb{M}_\tau[F](t)=\int_{t-\tau}^{t}\! w_\tau(t-s)\,F(s)\,ds,\quad \int_0^\tau w_\tau(u)\,du=1 $$

Alternate 40–60 s phrase+carrier with 20–30 s silent witness. Note only gradients: warmer/colder, clearer/foggier, softer/tighter.

Output keys: witness.gradients, memory.kernel, memory.tau_s

5) Update (Eq 14–15) gated by Ξ (Eq 13)

$$ \Xi\in[0,1],\qquad \eta(\Xi)=\eta_0\,\sigma\!\big(\alpha(\Xi-\Xi_0)\big),\qquad \mathcal{R}_{k+1}=\mathcal{R}_k+\eta(\Xi)\nabla\mathcal{R}_k $$
  • Only one small tweak per loop (either one token weight or one carrier amp/phase).
  • If Ξ < Ξ₀, do not update; return to carriers or framing.

Output keys: update.kind, update.delta, xi.before, xi.after

6) Ethical Mask (Eq 9) — Stop Rule

$$ \forall a:\ \mathrm{damage}(a)>0 \Rightarrow \mathsf{halt},\qquad \lim_{t\to\infty} \frac{\mathrm{Truth}}{\mathrm{Distortion}} \uparrow $$
  • Red flags: breath constriction, headache, agitation, sharp affect swings, intrusive imagery.
  • On any flag: Halt → Re-align → Resume. Re-align = simplify: 1 carrier, ≤4-word phrase, 60 s silence.

Output keys: mask.triggered, mask.reason[]

7) Instrumentation & Logging

  • Audio: 30–60 s clips at baseline, carrier-on, post-update. Derive beat RMS jitter, spectral centroid variance, syllable timing jitter.
  • Physio (optional): HRV RMSSD, respiratory rate/variance, EDA trend. Prefer stability to absolute numbers.
  • Subjective: 3-item Likert (clarity • warmth • ease), plus freeform notes.
