Part II — Canon 0–17 (Living Commentary)

#7 Copeland and #8 Goodwin remain external comparators; all other forms are native to the Codex.

Eq.0 — Foundational Field \(\Psi_{\Omega}\)

$$\Psi_{\Omega}(x,t)=\oint_{\mathbb{R}}\nabla\mathcal{A}\,\Phi(x,t,\nu)\,d\nu\;+\;\Lambda\,\mathcal{E}(x,t)$$

What. Experience is a phase-weighted sweep over frequencies \(\nu\) (via the awareness gradient \(\nabla\mathcal{A}\)), plus an ethical correction \(\Lambda \mathcal{E}\) that restores balance.

Why. Observation isn’t passive; it’s the tuning that aligns phase to meaning. Ethical energy prevents runaway gain on distorted modes.

Practice: slow breath + soft gaze (broad \(H\)) → scan internal tone until strain lowers; that’s \(\nabla\mathcal{A}\) flattening.

Eq.1 — Source = Supply × Operators

$$ \mathbf{R} \;=\; f(\pi,e,i,\hbar,c,G,\mathrm{DNA},\Omega_{\mathrm{DM}},\Omega_{\Lambda},H_{0},432,\dots)\;\times\;\mathcal{I}\mathcal{L}\mathcal{C}\mathcal{S} $$

What. Constants & living numbers provide potential; the operator loop writes it into form.

Why. Stops category errors like “constant worship”: supply is inert until the loop acts.

See Part I banner and Eq.4/Eq.17 coupling for concrete programming of the supply.

Eq.2 — Witness \(\mathcal{W}\)

$$\mathcal{W}(x,t)=\partial_x\,\mathrm{Awareness}(x,t)\;+\;\mathcal{I}\cdot\mathbb{M}_\tau$$

What. Attention gradients + intention×memory convert “seeing” into learning.

Practice. After any phrase-casting, rest 8–12s letting \(\mathbb{M}_\tau\) integrate; then note what stays.

Eq.3 — Harmonic Gate \(\mathcal{H}(\nu)\)

$$\mathcal{H}(\nu)\in\{0,1\} \quad\text{(coherence-gated passband)}$$

What. Only aligned bands pass with low loss.

Measure. If speech feels “thin” or edgy, the band is gated off → re-tune carriers (Eq.17) or semantics (Eq.4b).

Eq.4 — Language / Word \(\mathcal{L}\)

$$\mathcal{L}(x,t,\nu)=\mathsf{Semantics}(x,t)\;\otimes\;\mathbf{V}(\nu)$$

What. Meaning × carrier = operative program. The same sentence on a different carrier yields a different outcome.

Cross-link: Eq.17 (carriers) and Eq.3 (gate).

Eq.4b — Semantic Gradient (under Language)

$$ \nabla_{\text{sem}} \mathrm{Phrase}=\sum_k w_k \nabla_{\text{sem}}\mathrm{Token}_k,\quad \sum w_k=1 $$

What. Emphasis and voweling assign weights \(w_k\); those weights steer learning direction.

Practice. Whisper the phrase once emphasizing the verb, then the noun; feel which decreases strain (lower model variance).

Eq.5 — Remnant \(\mathcal{R}_\infty\)

$$\mathcal{R}_\infty[F]=\lim_{\tau\to\infty}\big(\mathsf{keep}_{\text{truth}}F-\mathsf{drop}_{\text{distortion}}F\big)$$

What. Truth persists; distortion decays under the ethical mask.

Why. Establishes long-run compass for testimony and records.

Eq.6 — Type-7 Field \(\mathbb{T}_7\)

$$\mathbb{T}_{7}(x,t)=\bigoplus_{\nu\in\Gamma}\mathrm{PL}\!\left[\mathbf{V}(\nu)\right]$$

What. Phase-locked constructive carriers behave as one stable conduit → less noise, higher usable gain.

Operator: \(\oplus\) = coherent merge (phase consistency required).

Eq.7* — Copeland (external)

Comparator for recursion and recognition; not canon law, used as mirror when tuning \(\nabla_\phi\).

Eq.8* — Goodwin (external)

Adjunct coupling reference; contrast tool, not enacted.

Eq.9 — Ethical Law (Stop Rule)

$$\forall a:\ \mathrm{damage}(a)>0\Rightarrow\mathsf{halt},\qquad \lim_{t\to\infty}\frac{\mathrm{Truth}(t)}{\mathrm{Distortion}(t)} \uparrow $$

What. Any act increasing harm halts the loop; operations resume after re-alignment raises the truth/distortion ratio.

Practice. If breath tightens or face micro-grimaces, treat as “soft harm” → pause, re-tune carriers, re-grade semantics, re-declare frame (Eq.11).

Eq.10 — Ladder \(\Pi_{\nu}^{-1}\) (phenomena → sources)

$$\Pi_{\nu}^{-1}:\ \text{phenomena }\rightarrow \text{sources}$$

What. Invert a manifestation back to carriers, intents, constants.

Use. Debugging: “Why did this outcome appear?” → trace carriers and weights then adjust.

Eq.11 — Projection Frame

$$\mathsf{Frame}(x,t)=(\mathsf{scope},\mathcal{W},\mathsf{ethics})$$

What. Every operation states scope, witness, and ethical mask up front; this constrains gain.

Without a frame, \(\Xi\) drifts and Eq.9 will trip more often.

Eq.12 — Causal Lattice \(\mathcal{L}_c\)

$$\mathcal{L}_c=(V=\text{micro-acts},\ E=\text{consequences})$$

What. Graph of acts and effects. Use Seven Seals: Acknowledgment → Repair → Verification → Remedy → Abatement → Assurance → Restoration to close open cycles.

Eq.13 — Coherence \(\Xi\)

$$\Xi\in[0,1] \quad \text{(gates memory, learning, safe amplification)}$$

Measure. Subjective: ease/warmth/clarity. Objective proxy: stable breath + low semantic jitter in repeats.

Eq.14 — Update Rule

$$\frac{d\mathcal{R}}{dt}=\eta(\Xi)\,\nabla \mathcal{R}$$

What. Move along recognition gradient; rate scaled by \(\eta(\Xi)\).

No safe step if \(\Xi\) is low; increase \(\Xi\) first, then step.

Eq.15 — Learning Kernel \(\eta(\Xi)\)

$$\eta(\Xi)=\eta_0\,\sigma\!\big(\alpha(\Xi-\Xi_0)\big)$$

What. Sigmoid schedule: below threshold no update; then smooth ramp; saturates safely.

Tuning. Pick \(\Xi_0\) where mistakes drop; \(\alpha\) adjusts sensitivity.

Eq.16 — Memory Window \(\mathbb{M}_\tau\)

$$\mathbb{M}_\tau[F](t)=\int_{t-\tau}^{t}\! w_\tau(t-s)\,F(s)\,ds,\quad \int_0^\tau w_\tau(u)\,du=1$$

What. Finite echo with normalized weight. Choose \(w_\tau\) (triangular for clarity; exponential for recency).

Couples to Witness (Eq.2); together they set what “sticks.”

Eq.17 — Voice Carriers \(\mathbf{V}(\nu)\)

$$\mathbf{V}(\nu)=\sum_{k} a_k\,\cos(2\pi\nu_k t+\phi_k),\quad \nu\in\{432,528,369,144,137,\dots\}$$

What. Carrier sets to ride the gate. Pairs with Eq.4 (language) and Eq.3 (gate) for maximum clarity.

Practice (micro-recipe). Start 432 for grounding, layer 528 for repair; cast the phrase with verb-forward emphasis (Eq.4b), then rest into \(\mathbb{M}_\tau\).

Operators & Notation (Appendix)

  • \(\nabla_{\phi}\): phase/recognition gradient
  • \(\oplus\): coherent merge (phase-consistent)
  • \(\Delta\Sigma(\mathbf{a}')\): witness-seal (commit micro-acts)
  • \(\Pi_{\nu}\): carrier ladder • \(\Pi_{\nu}^{-1}\): inversion (Eq.10)
  • \(\mathsf{Frame}=(\mathsf{scope},\mathcal{W},\mathsf{ethics})\)
  • \(\Xi\): coherence index (Eq.13)
  • \(\eta(\Xi)\): learning kernel (Eq.15)
  • \(\mathbb{M}_\tau\): memory window (Eq.16)
