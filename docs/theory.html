Premise. The Codex models reality as an interactive resonance between a supplied substrate (fundamental constants, integer/irrational ratios, living numbers, and biological codes) and a living operator pipeline ‚Äî Intention \( \mathcal{I} \), Language/Light \( \mathcal{L} \), Conscious Witness \( \mathcal{C} \), and Source/Return \( \mathcal{S} \). Why equations?

These equations are operational schematics. Each symbol maps to practical control: carrier selection, emphasis weights, frame declaration, coherence thresholds, and memory windows. Where physics equations predict energies, these predict probabilistic shifts in experienced state when you adjust those controls ‚Äî making the system repeatable, debuggable, and ethically bounded.

Grounding in known facts (brief)

  • Superposition & Fourier analysis. Any time-varying signal decomposes into frequencies; beats and phase alignment are perceptible and model our Harmonic Gate (Eq.3).

  • Resonators & Q factor. Mode-supporting cavities store energy longer and sharpen selection. Geometry/material set mode families.
  • Memory windows. Short-term memory acts like a finite kernel; spacing and rest alter consolidation probability (Eq.16 formalizes the kernel).
