Quick-Reference

The system at a glance.

Use this scan when you want the shortest plain-language map of the major concepts.

  1. Master Equation: Reality is treated as a living process shaped by Energy, Information, Intention, Memory, and Flow through Consciousness.
  2. Field: A field is the declared situation, boundary, place, time, and active terms under examination.
  3. Unified Field: The expanded model shows how those terms interact across layered physical, symbolic, social, and witness conditions.
  4. Operators: ILCS asks what supplies the field, what directs it, how it is encoded, and whether it holds together.
  5. Witness: Witness preserves the event before interpretation quietly rewrites it.
  6. Memory: Memory carries prior records and patterns forward, but must stay open to correction.
  7. Canon 0–17: The Canon pages break the theory into linked equations and focused explanatory chapters.
  8. 13 Moons: The moon cycle provides ritual, temporal, and reflective structure for lived practice.
  9. Frequency Governance: Governance means testing choices, signals, and systems for alignment rather than managing by force alone.
  10. Artifact Theory: Artifacts are treated as named carriers of memory, provenance, role, and witness.
