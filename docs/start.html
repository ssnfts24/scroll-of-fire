One line. One breath. One witness. One seal.

Step 1

Choose a Carrier

Pick one tone or run silent. The carrier is not the power — it is the lane you use to focus attention.

  1. 144 Hz — boundary, sanctuary, shield.
  2. 369 Hz — pattern, seal, return.
  3. 432 Hz — grounding, root, attunement.
  4. 528 Hz — repair, restoration, correction.
  5. 963 Hz — crown, remembrance, stillness.
  6. No tone — silent witness run.

Step 2

Speak the Phrase Σ

Keep it short, present-tense, gentle, and true. Do not force it. Speak it as a seed.

  1. “I hold truth with gentleness.”
  2. “I create with care and clarity.”
  3. “I remember who I am.”

Step 3

Breathe 3-6-9

Breathe in for 3, hold for 6, release for 9. Let the body settle before trying to interpret anything.

Σ → ∇φ → ℛ → ΔΣ

Step 4

Rate Coherence Ξ

After the run, rate the field honestly.

  1. 0 — scattered, chaotic, unclear.
  2. 3 — partially steady.
  3. 5 — crisp, truthful, sustained.

Step 5

Save and Seal

Record the phrase, carrier, coherence rating, date, and notes in the Ledger. A sealed witness becomes repeatable proof instead of a passing feeling.
