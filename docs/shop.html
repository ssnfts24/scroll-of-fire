Process

How Artifacts Enter the Shop

Each item moves through a simple registry path before release.

1 · ReclaimMaterials are found, salvaged, gathered, or repurposed.
2 · FormCopper, stone, glass, and structure are shaped by hand.
3 · NameThe piece receives a title, number, and collection role.
4 · DocumentPhotos, notes, materials, and story are entered into the record.
5 · ReleaseThe piece becomes available only when complete.
