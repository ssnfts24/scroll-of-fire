Process

How Artifacts Enter the Registry

Each piece moves through a simple path before public release.

1 · ReclaimMaterials are found, salvaged, gathered, or repurposed.
2 · FormCopper, stone, glass, and structure are shaped by hand.
3 · NameThe piece receives a title, number, and collection role.
4 · DocumentPhotos, notes, materials, and story enter the record.
5 · ReleaseThe piece becomes available, gifted, archived, or kept personal.
