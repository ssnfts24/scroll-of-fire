Forge Process

How an Artifact Enters the Registry

The registry is not only a shop. It is a record of origin, material, handwork, witness, and release.

1 · ReclaimMaterials are found, gathered, gifted, salvaged, or repurposed.
2 · ShapeCopper, stone, glass, and structure are formed by hand.
3 · NameThe piece receives its title, code, collection, and role.
4 · RecordPhotos, notes, materials, finish, and story enter the registry.
5 · ReleaseThe artifact becomes available, gifted, archived, or kept personal.
Slight variations are expected. Hammer marks, hand shaping, natural patina, and individual asymmetry are part of the artifact’s identity.
