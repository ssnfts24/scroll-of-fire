Forge Process

How an Artifact Enters the Registry

The registry records origin, material, handwork, naming, witness, and release.

1 Reclaim Material is found, gathered, gifted, salvaged, or repurposed.
2 Shape Copper, stone, sea glass, all are formed by hand.
3 Name One-of-one pieces receive a title, code, collection, and role. With possible future updates to each piece.
4 Record Photos, notes, materials, finish, and story enter the registry.
5 Release The artifact becomes available, gifted, archived, or kept personal. Every piece is made intuitively by design.
Handmade variation: Hammer marks, hand shaping, natural patina, and individual asymmetry are part of the artifact’s identity. Repeatable series such as TB remain recognizable, but no two hand-forged pieces are exact machine copies. While supplies last copper comes frome the QRI 16 V.A.R.S.E Reality Engine.
