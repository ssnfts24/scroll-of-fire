Pre-Publish QA Checklist

Use this checklist for every site update before publishing.

Phase 1 — Critical stability

  • Confirm no broken core links on Home, Theory, Hub, Shop, and Ledger.
  • Verify navigation opens/closes on mobile and keyboard.
  • Confirm page sections load without console errors.

Phase 2 — Accessibility and quality

  • Validate skip link, heading order, focus visibility, and button labels.
  • Check contrast and readability on desktop and mobile.
  • Ensure forms and controls work without a mouse.

Phase 3 — SEO and discoverability

  • Check title, description, canonical, and Open Graph tags on updated pages.
  • Update sitemap entries and robots sitemap location when pages change.
  • Verify internal links and redirects still resolve correctly.

Release notes and tracking

  • Ship in order: critical fixes → performance/accessibility → content/design.
  • Track: page speed (LCP/CLS), broken link count, and engagement on key pages.
  • Record what changed and what to monitor in the next cycle.
