Framework

Four clean pillars

Field & Attention

Attention changes what a person notices, chooses, repeats, and records. In the Codex, this is described symbolically as the awareness gradient.

Language as Code

Words organize thought and group behavior. Clear language reduces noise; repeated phrases can act like anchors for memory, action, and discipline.

Three Grids

Earth rhythms, human systems, and personal attention are treated as interwoven layers. The practical goal is steadier alignment, not spectacle.

Ethical Coherence

Consent, transparency, humility, and repair are guardrails. A practice that increases pride, pressure, or harm is treated as a failed practice.
